Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2025

At Verex Footwear, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or purchase our products.

1. Information We Collect

When you browse our website or place an order, we may collect the following information:

  • Name and contact details (email, phone number)
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Payment information
  • Product preferences and browsing behavior
  • IP address and device/browser information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Communicate order updates and support responses
  • Personalize your shopping experience
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share it with:

  • Payment processors to complete transactions
  • Shipping providers to deliver your order
  • IT and security providers to protect our platform
  • Legal or regulatory authorities if required by law

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill your order and provide support. You can request the deletion of your data by contacting us directly.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze site traffic, and deliver personalized content. You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

6. Security Measures

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. All transactions are encrypted using secure socket layer (SSL) technology.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Correct or update inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, contact us:

  • Phone: +1 (213) 555-0198
  • Email: support@verexfootwear.com

We reserve the right to update this policy from time to time. Please review it regularly.
